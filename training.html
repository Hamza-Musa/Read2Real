📚 Training Checklist

Here’s the full detailed list with examples. Practice these daily.

Core Principles

  • Smile warmly: Even a small smile changes how others feel around you.
  • Remember names: “A person’s name is to that person the sweetest sound.”
  • Show genuine interest: Ask about their hobbies, work, or family.
  • Give honest praise: Example: “I appreciate how thorough you were on this task.”
  • Avoid criticism: Criticism makes people defensive. Suggest improvements kindly.

Influence Techniques

  • Find agreement early: Begin conversations where you both say “yes.”
  • Appeal to nobler motives: Example: “I know you care about fairness, so…”
  • Dramatize ideas: Make stories vivid instead of plain facts.
  • Issue challenges: Sometimes people respond best to a fun, motivating challenge.
